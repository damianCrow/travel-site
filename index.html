Sydney is one of the most beautiful, hot and interesting cities i have ever seen. I visited for the first time at christmas 2013 for 2 weeks. Most of my time was spent on the nothern beaches but I also spent a few days in the city centre. The weather is warm the sea is blue and the food, though a little expencive, is generally very good. IF you can survive the 21 hour flight from London UK, I would recomend visiting Sydney (last visited December 2013).

London Is reguarded by many people as one of the best cities in the world. I moved to east Lond in winter 1996. I have mixed feelings about this city. London can be amazing on a hot summer days spent in a park with friends. Unfortunately summer days here are quite rare.
